Fajitas

Description

Fajitas are fun. Fajitas are a party. Let's go ahead and get into the recipe!

Ingredients

  • Fajita Chicken
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Stir fry the veggies.
  2. Season and add the meat.
  3. Stir fry both together.
  4. Warm tortillas in a pan.
  5. Let sit and cool before serving.
