Pepper Steak

Description

Making pepper steak can be a daunting task. Here's a recipe to make it all easier.

Ingredients

  • Pepper Steak
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Slice and cook Peppers
  2. Add steak into pan
  3. Add sauce of your choosing
  4. Boil Rice
  5. Serve
